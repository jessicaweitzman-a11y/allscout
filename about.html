Our Mission

Making computer vision accessible to everyone

Computer vision has historically required expensive hardware, custom machine learning models, and dedicated engineering teams. We set out to change that.

AllScout gives anyone — regardless of technical background — the ability to point a camera at a problem and describe what they want to detect. Our Vision AI Agent handles the rest.

Whether you're monitoring your front door, a parking lot, a factory floor, or a swimming pool, AllScout turns your existing cameras into an intelligent detection system with nothing more than a text prompt.

Our Values

What drives us

🎯

Simplicity first

If you can describe it, you can detect it. We obsess over making complex technology feel effortless for our users.

🔒

Privacy by design

Your camera feeds are yours. We process video with strict privacy controls and never store footage without your explicit consent.

⚡

Real-time or nothing

Delayed alerts are useless alerts. Our infrastructure is built for low-latency detection so you can act on what matters, when it matters.
