Smart Alerts

Built-in alerts for
common scenarios

Choose from AllScout's pre-built alert library — or build your own with the AI Agent. Either way, you're covered.

Package Protect

Stay on top of every delivery with real-time package alerts. PackageProtect notifies you when packages arrive, identifies the delivery company, and alerts you if a package is picked up — helping you track deliveries and prevent theft.

Store Traffic

Understand how customers move through your store with real-time traffic insights. Identify peak hours with 15-minute tracking, monitor checkout wait times, and schedule staff more effectively using real store data instead of guesswork.

Lawn Watch

Turn your Ring camera into a smart lawn monitor. LawnWatch detects trash, toys, and pet activity in real time, sending instant alerts so you can keep your yard clean and know exactly when and where pets are doing their business.

ParkHost

Turn your Ring camera into a smart parking monitor. Get alerts when vehicles arrive, leave, or occupy your space so you always know what's happening.

PoolAgent

PoolAgent turns your Ring camera into a smart pool assistant. Monitor water clarity, water levels, and debris in real time while keeping track of your pool cover, robotic cleaner, and service visits.
