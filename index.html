How It Works

Three steps to smarter cameras

No hardware upgrades. No machine learning expertise. Just connect, describe, and stay informed.

01

Connect your camera

Add any IP camera, Ring, Nest, Arlo, or RTSP stream. AllScout works with the cameras you already own — no new hardware needed.

02

Describe what to detect

Use the AllScout Agent to describe detections in plain English. Or choose from ready-made alerts like package delivery, license plates, and more.

03

Get instant alerts

Receive real-time notifications with a screenshot the moment something is detected. View your full alert history anytime.
